The founding campus of the 23-campus California State University system, PV University provides a comprehensive university education, granting bachelor’s, master’s and doctoral degrees in 300 areas of study – offered through its eight colleges. With more than 45,000 students and nearly 4,370 employees, PV University continues to be an essential partner in the economic, cultural and social development of Silicon Valley and the state, annually contributing more than 9,789 graduates to the workforce. The university is immensely proud of the accomplishments of its more than 550,000 alumni, 45 percent of whom live and work in the Bay Area.



Excellent facilities

The University has a well-managed investment programme to provide for new, improved and refurbished facilities. The PV University Arena offers both state-of-the-art facilities and an exceptional standard of universal accessibility. As well as providing excellent facilities for all areas of sports study, the Arena allows the University to offer unique services to local sport clubs in the community as well as elite and professional athletes.



High quality teaching

PV University has some of the most comprehensive programs in the state, taught by the most talented and dedicated faculty that you will find anywhere. Our innovative curriculum truly prepares students for success in the 21st century.
